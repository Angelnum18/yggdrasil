Cosmic World Tree

In Norse mythology the universe is represented by a flat disk. The disc is located in the branches of the cosmic ash tree Yggdrasil, which supports the nine worlds. And the great Norse god Odin passes through each one of them until he can reveal the secret of the runes. The different worlds remain united by their roots and branches. The relative position between them is symbolic as they are not in the same dimension. The tree shows the relationship between the different world-realms separated by dimensional planes.
